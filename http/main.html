This web-page provides supplementary information:

Calzone L., Gelay A., Zinovyev A., Radvanyi F., Barillot E. A comprehensive modular map of molecular interactions in RB/E2F pathway. 2008. Molecular Systems Biology 4:174 doi:10.1038/msb.2008.7 Link

Short Abstract:
The retinoblastoma tumor suppressor protein (RB1) negatively regulates transcription by sequestering major transcription factors, the E2Fs. Here, we developed a detailed comprehensive map of some proteins and published interactions involved in the regulation of RB, along with an alternative modular diagram that we derived from the consensus network.
