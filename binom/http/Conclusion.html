Conclusion

This diagram is a comprehensive representation of the molecular interactions regulating RB activity. We were able to integrate an important amount of information. However, the purpose of such a diagram would also be to exploit these data in order to understand the pathway and anticipate the behaviour of any kind of mutations in the cell.
Using clustering of relevant cycles of the graph, the network has been divided into modules in order to simulate mathematically the behaviour of the pathway (discrete and continuous modeling).
